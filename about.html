ABOUT

Mission

Our mission is to coltivate a profound bond between our guests and the wonders of the natural world by prioritizing conservation, sustainability, and community support. We are dedicated to preserving the rich biodiversity of the Osa Peninsola, creating immersive experiences that reveal the beauty and significance of the land, and promoting responsible stewardship of the environment. Through eco-friendly lodging, sustainable farming, and guided wildlife encounters, we aim to inspire each visitor to cherish and protect the planet, fostering a lasting impact for future generations.

Vision

Our vision is to be a beacon of sustainable living and ecological preservation, where nature and people coexist harmoniously. Ecos del Bosque strives to set a standard for environmental protection and self-sufficiency, creating a zero-waste sanctuary that enriches both the land and those who visit. By leading in biodiversity conservation, sustainable farming, and educational outreach, we envision a future where our lodge and wildlife reserve inspire global change and nurture a deep respect for Earth’s ecosystems.

Ecos del Bosque is a wildlife reserve, a sustainable finca development farm, and an lodge, all working in harmony to protect the environment.

Founded by passionate Costa Rican naturalists, we aim to lead in flora and fauna protection, ecological awareness, and the organic development of a sustainable business. We are committed to generating zero waste in the South Pacific.

To achieve our goals, we operate in three distinct ways:

  1. The Lodge - An eco-friendly retreat that offers comfort while minimizing environmental impact.
  2. The Wildlife Reserve - A protected area where visitors can explore pristine ecosystems.
  3. The Finca Project - A sustainable farming initiative focused on zero waste and responsible agricolture.

The Finca Project

At Ecos del Bosque, we believe that it's possible to live off the earth without harming it. Our goal is to produce all our food on-site. We don’t buy from supermarkets, and we avoid using pesticides on our crops. Instead, we partner with local farmers when we need something we can’t grow ourselves, ensuring that our food is always fresh and sustainable.

All organic waste is repurposed into compost and fertilizers for our crops, as well as food for our hens and chickens. It’s a cycle of life, sustainability, and zero waste!

Key Principle: Everything we do to nature, we do to ourselves. The earth’s wellbeing directly influences our own quality of life.

About the Lodge

Our lodge offers eco-friendly accommodations with only certified sustainable products. From biodegradable soaps to water-saving appliances, every detail is designed to minimize our environmental footprint.

We also encourage our guests to reduce electricity and water consumption. We provide guidelines and tips for eco-friendly practices during your stay.

The Natural Reserve

We take great care to protect the flora and fauna of our reserve. All guests are provided with a brochure detailing our guidelines to ensure everyone respects the environment during their visit.

We offer nature tours that have minimal impact on the environment, with small local entrepreneurs leading the way. These tours provide an opportunity to connect with the region’s natural beauty while supporting local communities.

Come, experience the beauty of Ecos del Bosque, and be part of our mission to protect and preserve this incredible natural treasure.

We’re not just a place to stay—we’re a movement. Join us in making a lasting impact on the environment and the world around us!

General Roles for Staying

  1. Check-in and Check-out Times: Check-in time is at 3:00 PM and check-out time is at 11:00 AM. Please respect these times to allow us to prepare the lodge for the next guests.
  2. Quiet Hours: To ensure a peacefol environment for all guests, please observe quiet hours from 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM.
  3. Respect Privacy: Please respect the privacy of other guests. Do not enter rooms or common areas without permission.
  4. Safety Measures: For your safety, familiarize yourself with emergency exits, fire exits, and other safety protocols provided at check-in.
  5. Lost and Found: If you find or lose any items, please notify the front desk immediately.

Saving Energy During Your Stay

  1. Turn Off Lights and Appliances: When leaving your room, please turn off all lights, fans, and electrical appliances. This helps reduce unnecessary energy consumption.
  2. Use Energy-Efficient Appliances: We have equipped the lodge with energy-saving appliances. Please ensure you use them properly to minimize energy use.
  3. Keep Windows Closed: When using air conditioning or heating, please keep windows and doors closed to avoid wasting energy.
  4. Minimize Water Use: Limit er time to 5 minutes and turn off the water when brushing teeth or shaving to conserve water and energy.
  5. Use Natural Lighting: During the day, take advantage of natural light instead of turning on artificial lights.
  6. Laundry: We encourage you to use towels and linens for more than one day to reduce laundry loads and conserve energy.

General Actions to Save the Planet

  1. Reduce, Reuse, Recycle: Please separate your waste into recycling, compost, and landfill bins provided throughout the lodge.
  2. Plastic-Free Policy: Avoid using single-use plastic. Bring a refillable water bottle, and use reusable bags and containers.
  3. Conserve Water: Water is a precious resource. Use it responsibly by limiting ers, turning off taps when not in use, and reporting leaks promptly.
  4. Eco-Friendly Products: We encourage guests to use eco-friendly personal care products and cleaning items that are free from harmfol chemicals.
  5. Local and Sustainable Products: Support our eco-friendly farm-to-table dining options by enjoying locally sourced and sustainably produced meals.
  6. Respect Wildlife: Do not disturb or feed wildlife. Please keep a respectfol distance from animals and plants, and leave natural habitats as you found them.
