OUR ROOMS



Our rooms are divided in 2 categories: Casitas Deluxe, and Domes. Each of them are unique and thought whether you look for confort, luxury, facilities, or a Costa Rican casita style of lodging.

Limited Units, only 2 at the time (January 2025); we are just getting started. We have limited units available, we have considered that every traveller must have a unique experience where privacy is the most important detail.

To meet those conditions each casita or dome is separated from each other by 100 meters.

Casita Manakin Deluxe

A Serene Forest Retreat
Immerse yourself in the tranquility of nature with this cozy, private room nestled in a lush forest. Spanning 52m², the space is designed for comfort and relaxation, accommodating up to two guests. The room features a luxurious double bed (queen); it is the perfect getaway for couples or solo travelers seeking a peaceful escape.

Enjoy your morning coffee or unwind in the evening on the private open terrace, offering breathtaking views of the forest. The room is equipped with exterior furniture, creating the perfect spot for stargazing or soaking in the natural ambiance.

Inside, modern amenities include Wi-Fi, a fully-equipped kitchen, a refrigerator, and a coffee maker for your convenience. Whether you're here for a peaceful getaway or an adventure-filled trip, this room provides the perfect base to explore and recharge.

Aracari Dome

Forest Glamping Dome
Experience nature without sacrificing comfort in our beautifully designed geodesic dome, nestled in the lush rainforest. With 40m² of thoughtfully arranged space, this dome is perfect for couples seeking a romantic escape or solo travelers looking for tranquility.

The interior features a spacious and luxurious king-size bed, ideal for restful nights after a day of jungle adventures. A full exterior kitchen gives you the freedom to cook and dine under the trees, enhancing your connection with the natural surroundings.

Step outside and enjoy serene forest views from your private seating area, surrounded by towering trees and the sounds of wildlife. It’s the perfect place to sip your morning coffee or relax with a good book as the sun sets.

Inside the dome, modern amenities ensure a comfortable stay: you’ll enjoy reliable Wi-Fi, a standing fan, and thoughtful touches like a hairdryer, a full kitchen setup, coffee maker, and a welcome selection of coffee, tea, and juice. Designed for up to 2 guests, this dome blends rustic elegance with eco-conscious living.

Seasonal Rates

Our rates vary by season and reflect the experience, privacy, and sustainability we offer at Ecos del Bosque. All stays include breakfast and access to the nature trails, river, and farm areas.

Season Months Rate (per night)
High Season December – April $225
Intermediate Season May – August $195
Low Season September – November $170

Note: All prices are based on double occupancy and include taxes. We do not offer rooms for more than 2 people. The minimum age is 18 years old.

Cancellation Policies

We understand that plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair while allowing us to plan and operate sustainably. Please review the terms for each season carefully before booking.

  • Cancellations made more than 30 days before arrival will receive a full refund.
  • Cancellations made between 15 and 30 days before arrival will incur a 50% fee.
  • Cancellations made within 14 days of arrival are non-refundable.
  • Changes to dates are subject to availability and may incur additional fees.

  • Cancellations made more than 14 days before arrival will receive a full refund.
  • Cancellations made within 14 to 7 days of arrival are non-refundable.
  • One free date change is allowed up to 10 days before check-in (subject to availability).

  • Cancellations made more than 7 days before arrival will receive a full refund.
  • Cancellations made within 5 days of arrival are non-refundable.
  • Flexible rebooking options available in case of weather-related issues or road closures.

Note: All cancellations must be made in writing via email to infoecosdelbosque@gmail.com and refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 3 business days. In case of no-shows, no refunds or credits will be granted.
