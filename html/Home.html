The Natural Hideaway!

Ecos del Bosque is a paradisical hideaway nestled in the
South Pacific of Costa Rica.

This privileged biodiverse reserve spans across 32 hectares
of primary forest where 1.5 kilometers of trails extend
under primal vegetation.

It offers a one of a kind connection with our natural
elements.
And it is often described by visitors as a place
to feel each heartbeat.

Besides, the reserve is located in an evergreen community
called Vergel (A Vergel is a garden with a variety of
flowers and fruit trees.).
It honors up to its name, and you will see how life
thrives here in all colors.



LOCATION

The Osa Region: A Living Eden

Nestled between the Pacific Ocean and the lush rainforest,
the Osa region is a sanctuary unlike any other—
a biological bridge connecting North and South America.
Home to 5% of the world’s biodiversity, this untouched
paradise invites you to step into a world where
scarlet macaws soar, jaguars prowl, and ancient trees whisper
the secrets of time.

But Osa is more than just a haven for wildlife.
It is a land rich in history, where
indigenous traditions intertwine with nature,
telling stories passed down through generations.
Walk its hidden trails, discover breathtaking
waterfalls, and experience the deep, harmonious
connection between the land and its people.

Are you ready to witness one of Earth’s last wild frontiers?
Explore the rooms and our guided tours, designed to
bring you closer to nature’s wonders.

we highly recommend this fascinating article:

GETTING HERE

By Plane

Fly into the Osa Region from San José with local airline Sansa Sansa Airline. The airport is just 30 minutes from the lodge, Palmar Norte Airport.
Otherwise, you can fly to Quepos (La Managua Airport). It is 1 hour and 30 minutes from the lodge.

By Car

Drive along the Costanera Sur highway. To get here, you need to pass Manuel Antonio, Dominical, and Uvita. Then take a left exit at Punta Mala and a 10-minute drive to the lodge. Follow these instructions from the Juan Santamaria Airport: See Instructions. A 4x4 vehicle is recommended for the best experience.

By Bus

Buses from San José and other cities stop in Uvita. We can arrange a taxi from there.

Private Transfer

You can contact EasyRide, Interbus, or Costa Rica Shuttle to organize private transportation. Additionally, we can arrange a taxi from our trusted provider, offering private service at your disposal. Your ride, your time. Osa's Waves Travel
