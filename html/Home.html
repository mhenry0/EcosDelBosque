The Natural Hideaway!


The Private Wildlife Reserve and Lodge Ecos del Bosque is a paradisical hideaway nestled in the Osa region. This privileged biodiverse reserve spans across 32 acres of primary forest where 6 km of trails extend under lush primal vegetation. A natural landscape untouched by humans, and our natural haven.

It offers a one of a kind connection with our natural elements. And it is often described by visitors as a place to feel at peace and feel each heartbeat.

Besides, the reserve is located in a community called Vergel (A Vergel is a garden with a variety of flowers and fruit trees.). It honors up to its name, and you will see how life thrives here in all colors.



LOCATION



The Osa Region

As the biological bridge between North and South America, Osa region is a unique haven of biodiversity. This lush paradise boasts a staggering 5% of the world's plant and animal species, making it a must-visit for nature enthusiasts. Beyond its breathtaking landscapes, Osa holds centuries-old secrets rooted in indigenous traditions.

Explore trails, learn about local cultures, and immerse yourself in the rich natural history of this extraordinary region. Ready to experience the magic of Osa?

Check out our listings for unforgettable accommodations and tours that will bring you closer to nature and its wonders. We strongly advise reading this article: The most biologically active area in the world is in Costa Rica.

GETTING HERE

By Plane

Fly into the Osa Region from San José with local airline Sansa Sansa Airline The airport is just 30 minutes from the lodge, Palmar Norte Airport.
Otherwise, you can fly to Quepos (La Managua Airport). It is 1 hour and 30 minutes from the lodge.

By Car

Drive along the Costanera Sur highway. To get here you have to pass Manuel Antonio, Dominical, and Uvita. Then take a left exit at Punta Mala and a 10-minute drive to the lodge, follow this instructions from the Juan Santamaria Airport See Instructions A 4x4 vehicle is recommended for the best experience.

By Bus

Buses from San José and other cities stop in Uvita, we can arrange a taxi from there.

Private Transfer

You can contact EasyRide, Interbus or Costa Rica Shuttle to organize a private transportation. Besides, we can arrange a taxi from our trusted provider. A private service, at your disposal. Your ride, your time. Osa's Waves Travel
