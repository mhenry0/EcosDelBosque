Wildlife Guided Tour Nature Walk Family Friendly Educational

Along the way, you’ll uncover the hidden lives of plants, birds, and animals, and learn how they all connect in a beautiful balance.

Twilight Nightwalk: Discover the Magic of the Night

Wildlife Guided Tour Nature Walk Family Friendly Educational

Embark on a mesmerizing journey as dusk settles and the wildlife of the forest awakens. This unique twilight walk is a chance to experience the wilderness in a whole new light—under the stars, with expert guides revealing the secrets of nocturnal creatures.

As the sun dips below the horizon, you’ll wander along moonlit trails, listening to the sounds of the night, from chirping insects to the distant calls of owls. Keep your eyes peeled for creatures that are most active after dark, such as bats, night birds, and other elusive wildlife.

This family-friendly, educational walk is perfect for nature enthusiasts of all ages. It’s a unique opportunity to see the world from a different perspective, learn about nocturnal ecosystems, and connect with the natural world in a serene and tranquil environment.

Time: It starts at 4.30 PM and it ends at 8:00 PM. Available during summer, December to April. It includes homemade popcorns, hot drinks, and biscuits. We provide you with a flashlight.

NOTE:

What to Wear: We recommend comfortable hiking shoes, and lightweight clothing.
