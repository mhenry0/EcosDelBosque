FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

What is the lodge's location and accessibility?
Answer: The lodge is located in Vergel de Punta Mala, and it is accessible by car. It is a 12-minute drive from the Costanera highway. It is recommended to have a 4-wheel drive car.

What is the best time of year to visit?
Answer: The best time to visit is during December to May when the weather is sunny and warmer. However, winter offers unique moments, especially for birdwatching. The lodge is closed from September to November due to heavy rains. But we welcome guests from December to August.

What are the accommodations like?
Answer: The lodge offers 2 rooms with fully equipped kitchens, hot tub, terrace, hairdryer, coffee machine, and tea. See rooms here!

Is the lodge family-friendly?
Answer: Yes, the lodge offers a guided natural history hike and an eco-farm activity.

What are the dining options?
Answer: The lodge does not have a restaurant (we hope to have one by mid-2024). However, we offer a half-pension package, which includes dinner at a nearby restaurant.

What wildlife can I expect to see?
Answer: We have documented the flora and fauna in the reserve. Find the list here.

Do I need to book tours in advance?
Answer: Tours at the reserve do not require advance booking, though we offer a lodging + tours package at a discounted price. For external tours, please contact us in advance for reservations.

Are there guided wildlife tours?
Answer: Yes, we offer guided wildlife tours led by experienced guides. Our tours include the Natural History Hike and Nightwalk Tour.

Can I go on a safari?
Answer: No, we do not offer safari-type activities.

Are there any other activities available?
Answer: Yes, we offer a variety of tours. See the full list here. If you're looking for something else, we can connect you with our providers.

Can I swim in the river or lake?
Answer: Yes, there is a pristine river running by the property.

Is there a pool or hot tub?
Answer: We do not have a pool to reduce water consumption and promote natural adventures. There are no hot tubs at this time.

Is there a spa or wellness center?
Answer: There is no spa, but we can arrange for a massage from a local provider. Please let us know in advance to schedule an appointment.

Is there Wi-Fi available?
Answer: Yes, we offer Starlink internet in your room.

Is there a gift shop?
Answer: Yes, we have a small gift shop featuring Costa Rican products, including t-shirts and tote bags from our brand "Natural Costa Rica".

Is there a laundry service?
Answer: Yes, we offer laundry services.

Do you offer half-pension plans?
Answer: Yes, we offer half-pension plans, including meals at a nearby restaurant, Tilapias el Pavon.

Is there a back-up battery in the room?
Answer: Yes, we provide backup batteries in case of electric outages during heavy rain.

What is the check-in and check-out time?
Answer: Check-in is at 2 PM and check-out is at 11 AM.

Is there a minimum age for guests?
Answer: No, but please be aware that you're at your own risk in the wildlife. We have insurance, but encounters with venomous creatures are possible.

What is the lodge's policy on pets?
Answer: Pets are not allowed to avoid disturbance to the environment.

What is the cancellation policy?
Answer: Rooms can be canceled up to one month prior to your arrival, after which they are fully charged. If you have a reasonable reason for cancellation (e.g., health-related), we will refund your money.

How can I make a reservation?
Answer: You can make a reservation online or by contacting us directly.
