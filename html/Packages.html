Adventure Packages


Explore the Hidden Wonders of the Osa Peninsula

At Ecos del Bosque, we offer unique and immersive adventure packages designed to help you connect with the natural beauty of the Osa Peninsula. Whether you're seeking thrilling hikes, birdwatching tours, or a relaxing day immersed in the rainforest, we have something for every adventurer.

Our adventure packages provide guests with the opportunity to experience the wild, untouched landscapes of our private wildlife reserve. Guided by local experts, you'll discover secret trails, hidden waterfalls, and exotic wildlife that can only be found in this remote corner of Costa Rica.

From morning hikes to evening nature walks, each adventure is designed to offer an unforgettable experience in the heart of nature. Every package includes a blend of outdoor exploration, relaxation, and the chance to immerse yourself in the local culture.

What does it include?

  1. Half-Pension Package Breakfast and Dinner or Lunch
  2. Free drinks and snacks on your arrival
  3. Lodging
  4. Private Tours
  5. Free cocktail welcome drink

Why are these packages suitable for you?

We offer a discounted price for clients who buy this packages before arriving. It is a money-wise decision since it is more expensive to do the private tours if not pre-arranged.
Therefore, we offer the option of having all organized by us.

Our Adventure Packages:

    The Golden Package offers the ultimate connection with nature through a curated adventure that showcases the Osa Peninsula’s most captivating secrets. Perfect for those seeking a truly unforgettable experience, this package combines exploration, relaxation, and indulgence.

    What does it include?

    It includes 4 private tours + dinner or lunch during your stay. The breakfast is already included.

    First a thrilling 1-Nightwalk Adventure, where you’ll witness the forest come alive after sunset and discover the fascinating behaviors of nocturnal creatures under the guidance of an expert naturalist. The 2-Natural History Hike takes you through lush trails, unveiling the unique flora, fauna, and geological wonders of the region. On the 3-Medicinal Plants and Farm Tour, you’ll gain valuable insights into the healing properties of local plants, and you will see firsthand how we work with the land.

    A highlight of the package is the 4-Hidden Waterfall Tour, where you’ll venture off the beaten path to uncover a secluded waterfall, perfect for a refreshing swim or simply soaking in the serene ambiance. To complete your experience, you’ll enjoy delicious meals, including dinner the first day and lunch the second day , along with a freshly prepared breakfast crafted from local ingredients to energize and delight you.

    This all-inclusive experience allows you to explore the natural beauty of the Osa Peninsula while savoring its tastes and tranquility. Book your Golden Package now to create memories that will last a lifetime!

    The Schedule

    The Nightwalk is done the first day. The second day we do the Natural History Hike, Medicinal Plants and Farm, and the Hidden Waterfall hike.

    Time

    The nightwalk takes 2 hours and it starts at 7 PM. The other 3 tours start at 6 AM with the Natural History Hike, it takes 2 - 2.5 hours (we can even start at 5.30 AM so that we can do early birding as well).

    Then, when we return we eat breakfast. After that we do the Medicinal Plants and Farm Tour for 1 hour. Finally, we head to the Hidden Waterfall Hike which takes 2 hours. It is an approximation, it will depend on the interest of the client and how long they want to stay at each stop (the waterfall), we can stay for 2 - 2.5 hours, it is a 25 minutes walk each way.

    Right after coming from the waterfall the lunch is included at a local restaurant, the best fried tilapia along the Pacific. At the place we also do a tour around to learn all about pisciculture: extra tour 5-The Agriculture of Fish Production.
