The Hidden Waterfall of Vergel



Just a short distance from our reserve, you will find a hidden gem—a magical waterfall nestled deep within the rainforest. Situated within the majestic canyon of the Vergel River, this serene spot is a true testament to the untouched beauty of nature.

To reach the waterfall, guests must pay a modest entrance fee of $10. From there, you'll embark on a scenic 25-minute walk along a trail that winds through second-growth forest. This hike offers a unique opportunity to spot various bird species that thrive in the dense undergrowth, vines, and shrubs of the forest. The path is a treasure trove for birdwatchers and nature enthusiasts alike.

While our reserve primarily features primary forests, this excursion provides a rare opportunity to explore the rich biodiversity of secondary forests, forest edges, and shrubland. It's a perfect contrast, allowing you to see how life flourishes in different stages of forest regeneration.

Throughout the tour, you will be guided by a knowledgeable local guide who will lead you through the riverside area, where you will pass beneath towering wild heliconias—a vital source of nectar for hummingbirds and bats. The forest’s sights and sounds create a truly immersive experience, offering visitors a deeper connection with the vibrant ecosystem.

One of the most fascinating features of this tour is the sight of unique natural formations—large stones shaped by nature to resemble meteorites or materials from outer space. These extraordinary rocks add a surreal element to the journey, making this tour a once-in-a-lifetime experience.
