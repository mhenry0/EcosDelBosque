ROOMS



Our rooms are divided in 2 categories: Casitas Deluxe, and Domes. Each of them are unique and thought whether you look for confort, luxury, facilities, or a Costa Rican casita style of lodging.

Limited Units, only 2 at the time (January 2025); we are just getting started. We have limited units available, we have considered that every traveller must have a unique experience where privacy is the most important detail.

To meet those conditions each casita or dome is separated from each other by 300 meters.

A Serene Forest Retreat
Immerse yourself in the tranquility of nature with this cozy, private room nestled in a lush forest. Spanning 60m², the space is designed for comfort and relaxation, accommodating up to three guests. The room features a luxurious double bed and a convenient sofa bed, perfect for families or small groups.

Enjoy your morning coffee or unwind in the evening on the private open terrace, offering breathtaking views of the forest and the ocean beyond. The room is equipped with exterior furniture, creating the perfect spot for stargazing or soaking in the natural ambiance.

Inside, modern amenities include Wi-Fi, air conditioning, a fully-equipped kitchen, and a coffee maker for your convenience. Whether you're here for a peaceful getaway or an adventure-filled trip, this room provides the perfect base to explore and recharge.
