Adventure Packages

Explore the Hidden Wonders of the South Pacific

At Ecos del Bosque, we offer unique and immersive adventure packages designed to help you connect with the natural beauty of the Osa Peninsula. Whether you're seeking thrilling hikes, birdwatching tours, or a relaxing day immersed in the rainforest, we have something for every adventurer.

Our adventure packages provide guests with the opportunity to experience the wild, untouched landscapes of our private wildlife reserve. Guided by local experts, you'll discover secret trails, hidden waterfalls, and exotic wildlife that can only be found in this remote corner of Costa Rica.

From morning hikes to evening nature walks, each adventure is designed to offer an unforgettable experience in the heart of nature. Every package includes a blend of outdoor exploration, relaxation, and the chance to immerse yourself in the local culture.

What does it include?

  1. Half-Pension Package Breakfast and Dinner or Lunch
  2. Free drinks and snacks on your arrival
  3. Lodging
  4. Private Tours
  5. Free cocktail welcome drink

Why are these packages suitable for you?

We offer a discounted price for clients who buy this packages before arriving. It is a money-wise decision since it is more expensive to do the private tours if not pre-arranged.
Therefore, we offer the option of having all organized by us.

Our Adventure Packages:

The Golden Package offers an unparalleled connection with nature through a carefully curated adventure that highlights the Osa Peninsula’s most captivating secrets. Ideal for those seeking a truly unforgettable experience, this package combines exploration, relaxation, and indulgence.

What does it include?

This all-inclusive package includes 4 private tours, plus dinner or lunch during your stay. Breakfast is also included each day.

1. Nightwalk Adventure

Embark on a thrilling 1-Nightwalk Adventure where you’ll witness the forest come alive after sunset. Discover the fascinating behaviors of nocturnal creatures under the guidance of an expert naturalist. This is a unique opportunity to explore the jungle’s night-time wonders.

2. Natural History Hike

The 2-Natural History Hike takes you through lush trails, revealing the region’s unique flora, fauna, and geological marvels. Gain a deeper understanding of the diverse ecosystems that make the Osa Peninsula so special.

3. Medicinal Plants and Farm Tour

On the 3-Medicinal Plants and Farm Tour, you’ll learn about the healing properties of local plants and see firsthand how we work sustainably with the land. This experience will deepen your appreciation for the natural resources that sustain this vibrant ecosystem.

4. Hidden Waterfall Tour

A highlight of the Golden Package is the 4-Hidden Waterfall Tour, where you’ll venture off the beaten path to uncover a secluded waterfall. Perfect for a refreshing swim or simply soaking in the serene ambiance, this is an unforgettable experience.

To complement these adventures, enjoy delicious meals, including a special dinner on the first day and lunch on the second day. Our freshly prepared breakfast, crafted from local ingredients, will energize and delight you each morning.

This all-inclusive experience allows you to fully explore the natural beauty of the Osa Peninsula while savoring its unique flavors and tranquil atmosphere. Book your Golden Package now to create memories that will last a lifetime!
