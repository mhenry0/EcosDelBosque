FARM

We firmly believe that it is possible to live in harmony with our planet by minimizing our environmental impact.

To achieve this goal at our lodge, we maintain an organic farm that produces a diverse range of homemade goods within a self-sustaining framework designed to minimize waste. This eco-friendly approach reflects our commitment to preserving the environment while providing fresh, locally-sourced produce.
